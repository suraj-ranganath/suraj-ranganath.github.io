Vizia

Bringing light to the Blind.

Scroll
Project Date October, 2017
Role Developer
Tech Stack Android, Google Firebase
Visit Web
Github

I built this in 10th grade at my school as a Social Action Project under Eqube.

Many visually impaired people in our society often feel deprived of various things that common people get. So, here's my attempt at bringing a glimmer of light in their lives.

It comes with a simple blind friendly interface consisting mainly of taps, swipes and voice recognition to interact with the features of the app. The app responds with voice rather than graphics. As of now, it can read out stories, identify objects in front of you and interact with you as a companion.
